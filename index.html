About Us

ENCHORRO COMPANY LIMITED is a limited liability Company incorporated in Kenya in May 2014 and dully licenced as a water provider of both Piped Raw and fresh bottled water tested and certified for both domestic animal and human consumption.

  • Water Transportation

    Enchoro Company owns 3 (three) trucks fitted with water tankers that can carry capacity of 30,000ltrs of water. The lorries capacities are 5,000ltr, 10,000ltr and 15,000ltrs and able to supply to all the water towers along magadi three times a day.

  • Chemical Analysis

    As per the requirements by the Kenya Bureau of Standards (KEBS), bottled water for human consumption must be tested for chemical contents. Our Bottled water has been tested by KEBS and the ministry of public health and sanitation to make sure that it is fit for human consumption

  • Filtration and Treatment

    The treatment plant utilizes three stages that use no chemicals, is simple to use, provide consistent pure water without any maintenance other than routine cleaning and cartridges replacement, cost effective to run and provide good results.
